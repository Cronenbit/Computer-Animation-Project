1. Origin: 
A policeman is investigating reports of missing people in the woods.

2. Transition:
The police man encounters an abandoned car alongside the road.He stops and he begin to search around the area.

3: Conflict:
Walking trough the woods, he begin to see a lot of dead animals. He starts to panick and runs, getting deeper and deeper into the woods. 

4. Resolution:
At some point he stops running and takes a breath thinking he is safe. But all around him there are giant branches and roots. Finally,
a big hole in the ground, with  teeth and flesh takes him to never be seen again.
